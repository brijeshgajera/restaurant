Sesame Sweet Balls

Price: $6

Dissolve the brown candy in 1 cup of boiling water and set aside to cool overnight. Place the rice flour in a large bowl. Make a well and add candy water all at once. Stir until the water is incorporated; the dough will be smooth but slightly sticky.
